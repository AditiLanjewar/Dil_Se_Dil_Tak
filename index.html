Kabhi kisi ko khone ka darr sataya hai?

Hota nahi hai jaise baat karte

karte jab aap ek alag hi zone

mein chale jaate ho, ek aisa

zone jaha aap kabhi jana

nahi chahte....



Waha jaa kar aapko aisa lagta

hai ki yaar, bas ye insaan mujhse

door na ho jaye, kuch bhi bardash

hai par is shaqs ka mujhse door

hona nahi.....



Aur us darr ki wajah se aap kafi

insecure hone lagte ho aur thode

se zyada possessive....



Par kya karein, ateet mein jab

har shaqs ne bharosa dila kar

chhoda ho to insaan insecure

hota hi hai, jitna marzi chahe

bharosa ho jaye....



Khone ka darr satata hi hai......
